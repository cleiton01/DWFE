Ice Bear

The polar bear (Ursus maritimus) is a carnivorous bear.

  • 1o- Polar bears are found in the frozen wilds of the Arctic, in Canada, Alaska (US), Greenland, Russia and Norway.
  • 2o- These are seriously big bears, gang. Adult polar bears can measure over 2.5m long and weigh around 680kg.
  • 3o- Did you know that a polar bear’s fur isn’t white? It’s actually transparent with a hollow core that reflects light. This helps the bears blend in with their surroundings – a useful trick, especially when hunting wary seals!

The polar bear is a synonym of purity and beauty. A beautiful animal that shows beauty on the ice.
